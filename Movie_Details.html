About Harry Potter

Harry Potter is an orphaned boy raised by his intolerable aunt and uncle. On his 11th birthday he discovers he is a wizard. Harry also beings to learn the dark secrets of his past, including that he survived a killing curse the same night his parents were murdered by the Dark Lord, Voldemort.
